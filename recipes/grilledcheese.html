Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Description

This is the world's greatest grilled cheese sandwich. Not only does it have cheese on the inside, but it also has cheese on the outside! How crazy is that? I'll answer you: pretty darn crazy!!

This recipe is pretty easy to make, I think. I haven't actually read it yet, but it's a freakin' grilled cheese sandwich, how hard can it be?

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Cheese
  • Butter
  • More cheese

Steps

  1. Slice the bread
  2. Put some cheese between two slices of bread
  3. Sprinkle some cheese on the outside of the bread
  4. Heat up a pan and throw some butter on it
  5. Put the sandwich on the pan
  6. Let it cook until it looks yummy, then flip it over
  7. Let it cook some more
  8. Done!
