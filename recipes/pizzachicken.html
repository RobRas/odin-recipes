Pizza Chicken

Description

This is a chicken with a pizza on top. What's not to love? It even has pepperonis on top! Holy cow!

The image shows broccoli with it, but that's icky and gross. Leave that to the herbivores, am I right? Of course I'm right.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup Italian-seasoned bread crumbs
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1/2 cup all-puropse flour
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 2 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce, divided
  • 1/2 cup shredded mozzarella cheese, divided
  • 4 slices pepperoni, or to taste - divided

Steps

  1. Get the oven hot
  2. Mix bread crumbs, parmesan cheese, salt, and black pepper. Put flour in a second bowl. Mix egg and lemon juice in a third bowl
  3. Chicken breast in egg mixture and flour. Then dip again! Put em on a baking dish
  4. Bake in oven for 20 minutes
  5. Put sauce on chicken. Put motz on chicken. Put pepperoni on chicken.
