Waffles

Description

These waffles were passed down my family line for generations. When grandma got the recipe from her grandma, who in turn got the recipe from her grandma, she knew that this was something special. Many lifetimes worth of experimentation and effort were put into making these the perfect waffle

These waffles are basic waffles. Nothing special. You know the drill.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 4 teaspoons baking powder
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1.5 cups warm milk
  • 1/3 cup butter, melted
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Beat the eggs. Stir in the milk, butter, and vanilla. Pour milk into flour and beat it.
  2. Mix flour, salt, baking powder, and sugar. Preheat waffle iron.
  3. Put the batter into the waffle iron. Cook. Serve.
